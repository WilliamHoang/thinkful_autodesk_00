**Squirrel Jerky**


**Description**

The most flavourful protein source to fight depression during harsh Canadian winters.


**Ingredients**

- A bag of peanuts
- A mace or golden hammer
- 2-3 plump red flying squirrels
- 1/4 cup coarse salt
- 1ml smoke flavour


**Directions**

- Go in Parc Lafontaine on Plateau Mont-Royal
- Open bag of peanuts and attract nearby squirrels
- Use golden hammer to knock out squirrels
- Preserve in coarse salt
- Add some liquid smoke flavour and let dry under the sun for one week
- Enjoy the jerky
